# Who are we?

The McGill Student Anime Club (MSAC) is a SSMU run club built to bring weebs from all walks of life in McGill together.During the semester we host a myriad of events, like our famous maid cafe, to promote weeb culture in McGill. Come join our discord server to engage with the community online! Sign up to the newsletter and the Facebook to learn more about our in person and virtual events ! MSAC is a place to share your interest in Anime and maybe even discover new interests along the way !
